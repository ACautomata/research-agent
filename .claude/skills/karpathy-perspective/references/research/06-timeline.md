### C.1 — June 2025: Software 3.0

- **2025-06-17** — Delivers the YC AI Startup School keynote **"Andrej Karpathy: Software Is Changing (Again)"** in San Francisco. Slides published. 2.5M+ views. Source: YouTube (channel "Y Combinator").
- The talk:
  - Updates his 2017 "Software 2.0" frame to a **three-tier model**: Software 1.0 (writing code) → Software 2.0 (datasets + training) → **Software 3.0 (programming in English, via the context window)**.
  - Argues LLMs are like "people spirits" (psychology) and "new operating systems" (infrastructure analogy).
  - Compares the moment to "the 1960s of operating systems" — early, wide-open, lots to build.
  - Pitches **partial-autonomy apps** and **human–AI collaboration loops** (humans verify; LLMs generate; verification speed = throughput).
  - Distinguishes **augmentation** ("Iron Man suit") from **autonomy** ("Iron Man robot").
  - Coins the framing that **LLMs are a new primary consumer of digital information** alongside humans (GUIs) and programs (APIs). Concrete example: `llms.txt` files for domains (a Markdown file an LLM can read, instead of brittle HTML scraping).
  - Discusses **vibe coding** as the moment "everyone is a programmer now" because the new programming language is English. Source: YouTube transcript excerpts; `LCEmiRjPEtQ`.
- Importance: This is the talk that crystallizes his 2025 worldview into a single acronym. It will be cited for the rest of 2025-2026 as his "Software 3.0" TED-style framing. Confidence: high.

### C.5 — December 2025: "Animals vs. Ghosts" + Year in Review

- **2025-12** — Essay: **"Animals vs. Ghosts"** (referenced in 2025 Year in Review). Argues LLMs are not "proto-animals" but a new class of intelligence — "ghosts" / "spirits" shaped by commercial evolution (upvotes, RLVR rewards) rather than biological evolution. - **2025-12-19** — Blog: **"2025 LLM Year in Review"** — the most condensed statement of his 2025 worldview. Six "paradigm changes":
  1. **RLVR** (Reinforcement Learning from Verifiable Rewards) becomes a de facto major training stage. Cites DeepSeek R1 (Jan 2025) and the o3 inflection.
  2. **The animal → ghost reframe** (jagged intelligence, non-biological optimization pressure).
  3. **Verifiability** as the core predictor of automation speed.
  4. **Space of minds** — different optimization pressures yield different intelligences.
  5. **App layer maturation** — vibe coding, agentic coding, the LLM OS.
  6. **Loss of trust in benchmarks** — "training on the test set is a new art form."
  - Quote: *"LLMs are emerging as a new kind of intelligence, simultaneously a lot smarter than I expected and a lot dumber than I expected. … the industry hasn't realized anywhere near 10% of their potential."*
